# Privacy Policy for SassySwipe

**Effective Date: February 19, 2026**  
**Last Updated: February 19, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **SassySwipe** ("we," "our," or "us"). SassySwipe is a finger exercise app designed to help users improve finger dexterity, strength, and coordination through fun, gamified touch-based workouts.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information when you use the SassySwipe mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how the App works.

---

## 2. Summary (TL;DR)

> **SassySwipe does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

- No account required
- No internet connection required
- No personal information collected
- No data sent to any server
- No third-party trackers or ads

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 What We Collect (Locally Only)

All data generated by the App is stored **exclusively on your device** and is never transmitted anywhere:

| Data Type | Description | Storage |
|---|---|---|
| Exercise Stats | Reps, scores, speed, accuracy | Local device only |
| Progress & Streaks | Daily streaks, session history | Local device only |
| Achievements & Badges | Earned rewards and milestones | Local device only |
| Difficulty Settings | Your chosen exercise level | Local device only |

### 3.2 What We Do NOT Collect

- No name, email address, or any personally identifiable information (PII)
- No location data (GPS or network-based)
- No device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID, etc.)
- No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
- No financial or payment information
- No health or biometric data
- No usage analytics sent to external servers
- No crash reports sent to third parties

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

Data generated within the App is used solely to:

- Show your exercise progress, scores, and statistics inside the App
- Track and display your achievements, badges, and streaks
- Adjust difficulty settings based on your preferences
- Provide session history and improvement tracking

**All of this happens entirely on your device. No data ever leaves the App.**

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only:** All App data is saved using Android's standard secure local storage (SharedPreferences / Room Database).
- **No Cloud Sync:** We do not back up data to any cloud service.
- **No Network Transmission:** The App does not make any network requests or send data over the internet.
- **Your Control:** You can delete all App data at any time via your device's settings.
- **Uninstall:** Uninstalling the App permanently removes all associated data from your device.

---

## 6. App Permissions

SassySwipe requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

| Permission | Reason | Mandatory |
|---|---|---|
| Vibration (`VIBRATE`) | Optional haptic feedback during exercises | No |

We do **not** request or use:

- Camera or Microphone
- Location (GPS or Network)
- Contacts or Call Logs
- Storage (external read/write)
- Internet access
- Any other sensitive system permissions

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

SassySwipe does **not** integrate with any third-party services, including:

- Analytics platforms (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel)
- Advertising networks (e.g., AdMob, Meta Audience Network)
- Social media SDKs (e.g., Facebook, Twitter)
- Cloud storage or syncing services
- Crash reporting tools (e.g., Crashlytics)

The App works completely **offline** and has zero external dependencies that process user data.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

SassySwipe is appropriate for users of all ages. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever, there is no risk of collecting data from children.

- We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
- We comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**.
- Parents and guardians can feel confident that the App is safe for children to use.

---

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

### Access
All your exercise data, progress, and statistics are visible directly within the App.

### Delete
To delete all App data:
```
Android Settings → Apps → SassySwipe → Storage → Clear Data
```

### Uninstall
Uninstalling the App removes all data permanently from your device.

### Opt-Out
There is nothing to opt out of — we do not collect or transmit any data.
